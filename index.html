Our Products Reach Us

Unaisa Tex is a modern sourcing office

Based in Bangladesh that specializes for sourcing apparel products

  • Making partnership to support customers to get competitive price & quality products from renowned factories by maintaining ethical & sustainable practices.
  • Focus on innovation, sustainability & customer service.
  • Have strong sourcing, Design support, operational support team to serve customer demand.
  • Committed to customer for on time delivery in a transparent way.

As we work toward greener worlds, we maintain sustainable factories as our production partners.
